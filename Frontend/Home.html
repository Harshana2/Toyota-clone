INSIDE TOYOTA LANKA

We are the exclusive dealer / distributor for the TOYOTA and HINO brands in Sri Lanka and a fully owned subsidiary of Toyota Tsusho Corporation (TTC), Japan. Hence, we are a global member company of the prestigious Toyota Group, Japan.Embracing the traditions and passing it on to future generations, while reaching global destinations, Toyota Lanka is the only automobile company in the country to be 100% owned by its true Japanese ‘parent’ company, the Toyota Group. This global backing makes Toyota Lanka a truly global corporate entity, contributing to its customers, corporates, society and country.
